1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Growbychat, a WhatsApp Business messaging and automation platform operated by Aashdeep Industrial Engineering.

Growbychat enables businesses to connect their WhatsApp Business accounts, manage customer communications, send approved WhatsApp marketing campaigns, automate messaging workflows, and monitor message delivery and engagement.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the Growbychat service:

  • Account information — Name, business email, and contact details provided during registration.
  • Meta authorization credentials — Temporary access tokens generated when you connect your WhatsApp Business Account via Facebook OAuth. We do not store your Facebook password.
  • Message transmission logs — Delivery status timestamps (sent, delivered, read) used to populate analytics within your dashboard.
  • Operational data — Configuration settings, automation triggers, and campaign metadata necessary for service delivery.

3. How We Process Meta & WhatsApp Data

Our platform uses authorized Meta and WhatsApp Business Platform integrations to facilitate:

  • Messaging and campaign management on behalf of your connected WhatsApp Business Account.
  • Automation workflows triggered by customer interactions.
  • Analytics and delivery tracking for sent messages.
  • Customer communication services including approved template messages.

Your customer lists and imported phone numbers are stored within encrypted databases managed by Aashdeep Industrial Engineering and are never shared, leased, or sold to third parties.

4. Information Security & Encryption

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted transmission — All data in transit is protected using Transport Layer Security (TLS 1.3).
  • Secure storage — Database records, customer information, and access tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Controlled access — OAuth tokens are automatically cycled and refreshed. Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel.

5. Your Rights & Access Controls

You maintain full control over your data and account:

  • Request complete erasure of your workspace, including contact databases, messaging history, and subscription metadata.
  • Request a full export of all records stored within your Growbychat dashboard.
  • Revoke our Meta page authorization at any time through your Facebook Account settings.
  • Disconnect your WhatsApp Business Account from the platform at any time.

6. Revisions to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, Meta guidelines, or applicable regulations. Revised versions will be published on this page with an updated effective date. We will notify you of material changes via email or through a notice on your dashboard.
